This study examines how international students in the United States seek information through the r/f1visa subreddit before and during the COVID-19 pandemic, revealing a shift from employment-related topics to shared concerns about travel, finances, and entry restrictions, alongside growing solidarity and emotional support despite persistent unmet information needs.
